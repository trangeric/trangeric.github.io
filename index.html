About

Hi, my name is Eric Trang. I graduated from UNLV with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering.

I have previous experience working in the MEP field of engineering before I decided to pursue product design. As a mechanical product designer I work on cosumer end products in the aftermarket automotive field.
